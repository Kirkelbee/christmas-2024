2014 : The first Christmas

We were very busy with our series of firsts.

We made our way down to Sedgefield via Philippolis where we discovered the Boer war remained in progress, the last of the Bittereinders (our host) holding out by trying to promote Afrikaans businesses over English ones!

I introduced you all to my family's famous Christmas cakes! I think our 10 year streak ended this year when we couldn't travel through PMB to collect!

You took me to Gericke's which has become something of a favourite place. No trip to Sedgefield is complete without a climb to the point and a swim in the tranquil pools below.

We made our first trip to Oudshoorn together, seeking sunshine after too many cloudy days! We visited the amazing Cango Caves

We finished up at Woodridge. Just yesterday Joel was talking about fetching the braai on the school bakkie, and I reminded him how Simon and I saved that wet wood fire in style!

I loved every minute of it! But there's more!
